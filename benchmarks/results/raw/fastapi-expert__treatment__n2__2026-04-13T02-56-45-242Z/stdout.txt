**How the N+1 fix works:** `selectinload(User.posts)` issues exactly 2 SQL queries regardless of user count — one `SELECT * FROM users` and one `SELECT * FROM posts WHERE user_id IN (...)`. Without it, lazy loading would issue 1 + N queries (one per user).
